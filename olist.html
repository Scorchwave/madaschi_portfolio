Olist Marketplace Dashboard

Power BI Case Study

End-to-end e-commerce analytics project focused on revenue performance, customer behavior, logistics efficiency and seller performance using real marketplace data.

Business Challenge

Marketplace operations generated large volumes of transactional data spread across multiple tables. The objective was to transform raw data into a clear decision-making dashboard.

What I Built

Interactive Power BI solution with KPIs for revenue, orders, customer growth, delivery performance, freight costs and seller activity.

Tools Used

Power BI, Power Query, DAX, Data Modeling, Data Cleaning, Business Analysis.

Key Insights

• Delivery delays negatively impact review scores.
• São Paulo drives the highest order volume.
• Freight costs significantly affect profitability.
• Customer retention opportunities exist after first purchase.
